Track HSL buses, trams, and trains in real-time. See exactly where your ride is on the map and never miss a connection in Helsinki!

DESCRIPTION:
Linjalla is your ultimate companion for public transport in the Helsinki region. Designed for simplicity and speed, it provides real-time tracking for HSL buses, trams, and trains.

Key Features:
• Real-time Map: Watch buses and trams move smoothly across Helsinki as they navigate their routes.
• Smart Search: Easily find and subscribe to the specific lines you use most.
• Favorites: Keep your frequent lines just a tap away for quick access.
• Live Departures: View real-time departure boards for major hubs like Rautatientori and Helsinki Central Station.
• Native Experience: Built with SwiftUI and MapKit for a fluid, battery-efficient, and modern iOS experience.
• Privacy First: No tracking, no ads, just the transit data you need.

Whether you're a daily commuter or just visiting Helsinki, Linjalla gives you the confidence of knowing exactly when your ride is arriving.
